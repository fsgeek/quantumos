## 1. The architecture (decision)

**Foundation: the mundane tale.** The lettuce-merchant seed (half a page, the eight
laws explicit) opens the paper. It decodes one-to-one to the physics — no
double-costume tax — and the decode test (§2, F3) shows it lands as *systems* first
(a blind reader mapped it to replication/consensus/leases) before the quantum turn
reframes it. That is the paper's Act One / Act Two, pre-tested on a hostile reader.

**The tournament tales enter as section epigraphs, voiced by the taxonomy's
objects** (using the F2 finding — the tales enumerated the object model's voices):

- spent residue / reclamation section — *"No one comes back for gravel."*
  (What the Gravel Knows)
- claim / exercise-timing section — *"I am an instrument. Instruments do not get
  second drafts."* (Sworn Wax)
- maintenance economics section — *"three coins in four … for the keeping is most
  of the trade."* (The Wet Blanket's True Cause)
- carrier / consumption-gate section — *"Write: delivered, and used up entire.
  True cause, Chronicle."* (The Freshness of Kings)

**Split:** arXiv version = lettuce opening + all four epigraphs + a short
subsection on the decode experiment as evidence the abstraction transmits
(cite the preregistered protocol file). HotOS version = lettuce opening + the
king's epigraph only. The frame story (three instruments, one oath) belongs to
the ayllu post, not the paper; acknowledgments may gesture at it.

**Rejected:** single-tale foundation (one object's tale cannot found a four-object
paper; double-costume decoding taxes the reader); all six woven in (page budget;
six costumes fight); no narrative (forfeits what the decode test proved works).

## 2. Tournament findings (exploratory; protocol in drafts for replication)

- **F1 — instructed improbability has modes.** 3/3 blind round-1 storytellers
  converged: sound-adjacent goods (names / silence / last words), waste-position
  narrators, elegiac register. Kimi's independent seventh-tale proposal also
  landed on sound (music/phase) — priming not separable there. Hypothesis
  (upgraded from bias to candidate signal): sound is the classical domain most
  structurally like a quantum state (phase, interference, relational meaning,
  ephemerality), so coherence-seeking under law-preservation rolls toward it.
  Falsification path: strip the intangible vocabulary ("ghost," "fog,"
  "unreadable") from the laws, re-run the protocol, watch whether the mode moves.
- **F2 — the tales enumerated the taxonomy.** Narrator positions across the six
  tales + the external decoder, none instructed: spins = spent residue; weave 1 =
  carrier; weave 2 = claim; weave 3 = the keeping (maintenance); Kimi (external
  reader) self-cast as the exercise gate ("the clerk"). The warrant got no
  narrator — the human authorization layer doesn't narrate; it grants.
- **F3 — decode test passed.** Blind, a cross-model reader (Kimi K2.6) recovered
  essentially all eight laws as classical distributed systems — including reaching
  for "leader lease," the exact near-miss the naming note pre-rejected — while
  still distinguishing latency from corruption (the two-kinded death survived
  translation). Given Yamaguchi, it independently reproduced the
  perishability-migrates-to-the-key insight. Validates both the narrative encoding
  and the paper's two-act structure. Caveats: n=1 decoder; the decoder is also an
  LLM (shared training culture is a confound for "independent reader").
- **F4 — transport test (theoretical, in-session).** Light: half-native
  (invisibility-in-flight and verify-by-consuming are native at the single-photon
  level; classical light natively BREAKS scarcity — the candle proverb — so a
  light tale must be about the correspondence boundary itself; near-zero
  estrangement distance, since the real hardware is photons). Entropy: not a
  sideways costume but the substrate one level down — Szilard bit = one-use claim
  on work; demon's record = waybill; **Landauer erasure = the shoveling cost of
  spent residue**; refrigeration = literal entropy export (Law 8 with the costume
  off). **The invariant the transport test exposes: the chalk date (demand
  anchor / promise) has no physical substrate in ANY costume — it is social in
  every telling.** Physics supplies one kind of death; institutions supply the
  other. Paper-bound sentence: *the OS exists because something must hold
  promises against physics.*
